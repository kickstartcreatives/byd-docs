Accent orange — use for
· CTA buttons (light text on orange)
· Decorative dots and lines
· Border-left highlights on cards
· Underline accents on key text
· Progress bar fill
· Display text 24px or larger
· The line/dash before a label
Accent orange — never use for
· Body text at any size
· Labels or eyebrows under 24px
· Step numbers or metadata
· Any text where contrast is needed
· White text on orange buttons
· Backgrounds behind dark text
